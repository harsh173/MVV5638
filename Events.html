Are you new to Townsville? Do you love music with a passion?

Then come and join the Townsville Community Music Center NOW! You can become a Registered user for FREE!

By becoming a Registered User, you can submit and edit notices for your favorite music band or events.

Once you become a registered member you can choose to upgrade to become a Paid Member.

By becoming a Paid Member, you can submit/edit notices and artists.

No matter what your age or taste in music, the Townsville Community Music Centre has something for you.
