CP5638- 2018 - TCMC Project Plan

Townsville City Council website

Members: Harshish , Praneeth, Karthik

Mission Statement

The new website must bring the music council close to the people. Increase the number of the new memberships, increase the ticket sale, encourage the musicians

Success Evaluation

The online is a big market where the sale or information spreads all over the world. The website is expected to the achieve the initial goals and may achieve the addtional goals like support from individual or organisation who are interested. The website might considerably increase the new membership rate and ticket sale rate

Target Audience

Target audience is people passionate about music especially the local and wider community, generally people who don't have time and eager to know the information and want to participate by online means, having a current or historical background in music. this would a great opportunity for the people who are eager to perform and watch the shows i.e. current/ex musical performers, music teachers, students, technicians, dancers.
